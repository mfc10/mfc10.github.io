Learn More about AmeriCorps Alums and Our Chapter Leader Network

AmeriCorps Alums is the only national organization connecting the nearly one million alumni of all AmeriCorps programs to the ideas, resources, and people that support a lifetime of service. We are a community of engaged citizens and civic leaders. The chapter network includes all the recognized alumni chapters across the country led by local chapter leaders who are supported by our National Chapter Council Representatives in addition to the Alums staff.

Get Involved

AmeriCorps Alums Chapter Leaders stay connected to the national organization and each other in three main ways.

  1. Following AmeriCorps Alums on Our Blog and Social Media. You can Find Us on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn.
  2. Subscribing To and Posting To Our Chapter Leader Google Group
  3. Attending Chapter Leader Training Calls (Dates Announced through Our Google Group).
  4. Connecting with Our National Chapter Council Representatives to Share Updates and Request Support. Find Your NCC Rep here!
  5. Submitting Monthly Chapter Reports

If you ever have any ideas, questions, stories or updates to share with AmeriCorps Alums national office, contact us at 404-979-2900 and ask for AmeriCorps Alums or email us at Chapters@AmeriCorpsAlums.org.

Update Chapter Records or Share News, Stories, and Pictures

We love hearing about your events, news, and great chapter stories. If you have an event, please be sure to post it to the AmeriCorps Alums Community Calendar. In addition, subscribe Chapters@AmeriCorpsAlums.org to receive your newsletter. If you have an awesome event or a great story from a chapter member, we want to hear about it and spotlight it. Click here to submit a great story to be considered for the Chapter Spotlight series on our blog.

Update Your Chapter Leader Contact Information

Has your chapter leadership changed recently? Please update your National Chapter Council Reporesentative with the new chapter leader name and contact information. In addtion, send an email to Chapters@AmeriCorpsAlums.org so we can update our records. We love to send outgoing chapter leaders a thank you note as well as welcome letter to the new leaders.
