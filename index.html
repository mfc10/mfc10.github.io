Connect with AmeriCorps Alums and Our Chapter Leader Network

Learn More

AmeriCorps Alums is the only national organization connecting the nearly one million alumni of all AmeriCorps programs to the ideas, resources, and people the support a lifetime of service. We are a community of engaged citizens and civic leaders. The chapter network includes all the recognized alumni chapters across the country led by local chapter leaders who are supported by our National Chapter Council Representatives in addition to the Alums staff.

Get Involved

AmeriCorps Alums Chapter Leaders stay connected to the national organization and each other in three main ways.

  1. Following AmeriCorps Alums on Our Blog and Social Media (You can Find Us on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Intstagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn).
  2. Attending Chapter Leader Training Calls
  3. Connecting with Our National Chapter Council Representatives to Share Updates and Request Support.(Find Your NCC Rep here!)
  4. Subscribing To and Post To Our Chapter Leader Google Group
  5. Sharing Monthly Chapter Reports

If you ever have any deas, questions, stories or updates to share with AmeriCorps Alums national office? Contact us at 404-979-2900 and ask for AmeriCorps Alums or email us at Chapters@AmeriCorpsAlums.org

Share News, Stories, and Updates with AmeriCorps Alums

Update Your Chapter Leader Contact Information

Has your chapter leadership changed recently? Please updated your National Chapter Council Reporesentative with the new chapter leader name and contact information. In addtion, send an email to Chapters@AmeriCorpsAlums.orgso we can update our records. We'd love to send outgoing chapter leaders a thank you note as well as welcome letter to the new leaders.

Share News, Stories, and Pictures

We love hearing about your events, news, and great chapter stories. If you have an event, please be sure to post it to the AmeriCorps Alums Community Calendar. In addition, subscribe Chapter@AmeriCorpsAlums.org to receive your newsletter. If you have an awesome event or a great story from a chapter member, we want to hear about it and spotlight it. Click here to submit a great story to be considered for the Chapter Spotlight series on our blog.
